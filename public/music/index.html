Music

Our Musical Universe

Silent Souls operates in the liminal space where darkness meets light, where underground meets transcendent. Our music is designed to take listeners on journeys through different states of consciousness while maintaining the primal connection to rhythm that makes electronic music so powerful.

Latest Releases

Dark Frequencies EP (2024)

Our latest offering explores the intersection of melodic techno and psytechno. This four-track EP showcases our evolution as artists while staying true to our underground roots.

Tracklist:

  1. Dark Frequencies (Original Mix)
  2. Dark Frequencies (Extended Version)
  3. Shadows in Motion (Club Mix)
  4. Infinite Loop (Atmospheric Remix)

Ethereal Void (2024)

A standalone psytechno journey that pushes the boundaries of conventional electronic music. Built around a hypnotic groove that evolves over eight minutes of sonic exploration.

Midnight Protocol (2023)

Our tribute to classic techno, featuring raw industrial elements and an uncompromising drive that has become a favorite in underground clubs worldwide.

Musical Categories

Each Silent Souls release falls into one of our core musical territories, though we often blur the lines between genres to create something entirely new.
